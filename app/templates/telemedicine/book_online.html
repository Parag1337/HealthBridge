Online Consultation Terms and Conditions
1. Scope of Service

Online consultations are suitable for non-emergency medical advice, follow-up consultations, and general health inquiries.

2. Limitations
  • Not suitable for emergency situations - call emergency services if needed
  • Physical examination limitations may require in-person follow-up
  • Prescription of controlled substances may not be available
3. Technical Requirements

You are responsible for ensuring adequate internet connection and compatible devices.

4. Privacy and Security

All video consultations are encrypted and comply with healthcare privacy regulations.

5. Recording Policy

Consultations may be recorded for quality assurance and medical record purposes with your consent.

6. Cancellation Policy

Online consultations can be cancelled up to 2 hours before the scheduled time.
